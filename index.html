Welcome To Clogher Road Community College DnD Blog

This is Clogher Road Community College's Dungeons & Dragon Blog, where students of Clogher Road Community College write about their adventures of playing Dungeons & Dragons together in the presepctive of their characters in the game


What is DnD?

DnD stands for Dungeons and Dragons. DnD is a roleplaying tabletop game made by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson. Players play as their own made up characters and one player called a Dungeon Master creates scenarios and situations all other players have to go through.

About the DnD Club in Clogher Road Community College

The DnD club was setup by our librarian Neil Kettles. We typically play a couple of hours once a day each month and pick up from where we left of in the next month. There are two seperate groups that play each month. Harry is our Dungeon Master.
